❓ FAQ
Common Questions
Does my data get sent anywhere?

Absolutely not. SanitizeOS runs entirely in your browser using vanilla JavaScript. There is no backend, no API, no server, and no network request made with your data. You can even download the file and run it completely offline.

What PII types does it detect?

Currently: US Social Security Numbers (SSN), credit card numbers (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), email addresses, and US/UK phone numbers. We're adding custom keyword redaction soon.

What's the difference between the free demo and paid version?

The free live demo lets you redact text you paste manually — great for quick sanitization. The paid version ($25 one-time) unlocks file uploads (.txt/.csv), bulk processing, and the download function.

Is this GDPR / HIPAA compliant?

Since data never leaves your device, SanitizeOS removes the cloud upload vector that most compliance frameworks address. However, compliance is a holistic practice — always consult your compliance officer for your specific use case.

What happens after I pay?

You'll receive a download link via Gumroad for the unlocked version of the single index.html file. No account needed — just download and use. Forever. Updates are included.

Can I use this on an air-gapped machine?

Yes! That's one of the core design goals. Download the file, move it to any machine, and it works with zero internet connection required.
